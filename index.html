#you-snake-we-snake

Build a unique Snake-inspired game,
get a large snake toy!

Step 1

Build a unique Snake-inspired game

Step 2

Submit your game for review

Step 3

Get an actual 45 inch snake toy after 6 hours

What's Snake?

Snake is a classic game where you control a growing line that eats food and gets longer. If you crash into yourself, game over.

Image via Wikipedia

How does this work?

Make a game inspired by the classic game, Snake.
Projects must include an aspect that makes it unique, not a clone.
After 6 hours, you can get a 45 inch snake plush toy!

Need inspiration?

Click the button to generate a random game idea.

Can I double dip?

Hours cannot be shared with other programs.

How do I track time?

You must use the Hackatime extension to track your hours.

Who can participate?

You must be teenager aged 13 to 18 in Hack Club.

When will this start?

To be determined.

Can I use AI?

Minimal AI is allowed for learning and troubleshooting, but you cannot build the majority of your project with AI. AI use must be disclosed. Projects that heavily use AI, or appear vibe coded, will be rejected.
